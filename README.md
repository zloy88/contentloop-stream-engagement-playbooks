# Streamer Engagement Playbooks (by ContentLoop)

This repository contains practical playbooks and templates for streamers
who struggle with quiet chat, dead air, or low-energy moments during live streams.

## What is Streamer Stall?
Streamer Stall is the moment when:
- chat stops talking
- energy drops
- the streamer has to improvise live

These playbooks document proven ways to keep chat involved
without begging, spamming polls, or burning out.

## Playbooks
- Streamer Stall Recovery
- Subathon Engagement Formats
- Quiet Chat Participation Ideas
- Viewer-Controlled Content Loops
